Welcome to my space.

Xiaowei is a Ph.D. candidate (started at Sep. 2017) in fluid mechanics under the supervision of {Prof. Richard Sanberg}(https://findanexpert.unimelb.edu.au/profile/742813-richard-sandberg) at the Unimelb Fluid Mechanics Group.

I received a MPhil. in Bridge Engineering from Tongji University (同濟) supervised by [Prof. Yaojun Ge]{https://bridge.tongji.edu.cn/Data/View/279} and Prof. Yongxin Yang in 2017. Before that, I received a B.Sc in Civil Engineering from Hunan University (嶽麓) under the supervision of Prof. Zhi Fang in 2013.

I am interested in turbulence modelling, machine learning, and bridges and wind turbines. I am fond of reading books about history, philosophy and films.

Feel free to contact me via: Github, and xiaoweix2@student.unimelb.edu.au.
